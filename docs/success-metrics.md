# Success metrics (MVP)

## Reliability
- Delivery success rate: >= 99% of due reminders result in a DM being sent
- Timeliness: 95% of reminders sent within 60 seconds of due time (cron frequency dependent)

## Parsing quality
- Parse success rate: >= 80% of reminder attempts result in a created reminder
- Unknown input rate: <= 20% "help" responses to reminder attempts

## Engagement
- Activation: >= 50% of users who DM the bot create at least 1 reminder
- Retention (early): >= 20% of users create a second reminder within 7 days
